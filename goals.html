Personal Bio - Goals

Career Goals

My main goal for my career is to become my own boss. Through the means of software engineering and coding and wherever my life takes me.

In regards to software engineering, my goals is to become a freelance engineer. It gives me various benefits such as:

  • There will be a lot of variety in the work I do
  • I can achieve a better work/life balance by deciding when I work
  • If I work hard enough, there is a constant stream of work
  • I am able to travel and experience new cultures more due to the nature of my work(more about this on my next goal)

Other than that, I am always eager to learn new things and gain new experience therefore even with a goal set, I am willing to go wherever I am attracted to in the years to come.

Travelling, Culture and sports

I am extremely fascninated by people, their stories and culture, and where they come from. To experience this I have a huge desire to travel to most countries around the world. Not only to travel but live there and experience their life in an authentic way. This will requires a high ammount of dedication towards my career as a high income and various planning is needed. This, however, is one of the motivation that keeps moving forward in terms of where I am in my life and career.

Other than that, I want to start partaking in extreme sports and activities. My expereince with trekking and hiking during my gap year ignited a new interest which I didnt know I had. Whislt travelling/experiencing new culture, I would like to test my limits and challenge my fears. Do things such as bunjee jumping, paragliding, white water rafting, sky diving and many more that pushes my body and mind to the extreme. I am lucky to have most of these facilities readily available around me. I am in the process of planning to embark on this journey very soon.

Musical Goals

Music has always been a huge part of my life. My grandad being a poet, musician himself and my sister being exeedling talented in piano and singing, it was all around me since childhood. Apart from being my hobby(go to hobby section above) I want to make something more out of music. I love performing and providing music so alongside my career, I have goals to make music a recurring part of my life. Performing in gigs and writing my own music would be what people say "dream come true".

Along side this, further along in my life when I am completely settled, I would like to open my very own music studio and work around other individuals with similar interests as me.
